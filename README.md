# TooliForge AI Workplace Assistant  
### *“Automating work. Amplifying productivity.”*

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**TooliForge AI Workplace Assistant** is a modern, AI-powered SaaS web application designed to streamline everyday workplace tasks and reduce administrative workload for professionals.

It acts as a unified productivity hub where users can generate emails, summarise meetings, plan tasks, conduct research, and interact with an intelligent workplace chatbot.

The platform demonstrates practical business value by transforming time-consuming manual workflows into fast, structured AI-assisted outputs.

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- Graduates entering the workforce  
- Administrative professionals  
- Team leaders and managers  
- Business analysts  
- Remote workers  
- Knowledge workers across industries  

---

## ⚙️ Core Features

### 1. Smart Email Generator
Creates professional emails based on user input.

**Capabilities:**
- Tone selection (Formal, Informal, Friendly, Persuasive, Professional)
- Audience selection (Client, Manager, Team Member, Stakeholder)
- Subject line generation
- Editable output before copying
- Copy-to-clipboard functionality

---

### 2. Meeting Notes Summariser
Transforms raw meeting notes into structured insights.

**Outputs include:**
- Key discussion points
- Decisions made
- Action items
- Deadlines
- Responsible persons

Supports export-ready summaries for documentation.

---

### 3. AI Task Planner & Scheduler
Helps users organise and prioritise tasks intelligently.

**Features:**
- Task categorisation:
  - Urgent & Important
  - Important but Not Urgent
  - Urgent but Less Important
  - Low Priority
- Daily and weekly schedule generation
- Suggested task ordering
- Estimated time allocation
- Productivity optimisation tips

---

### 4. AI Research Assistant
Converts complex content into simplified insights.

**Capabilities:**
- Executive summaries
- Key insights extraction
- Recommendations
- Simplified explanations
- Highlighting important findings

Supports pasted text, articles, or research prompts.

---

### 5. AI Workplace Chatbot
A conversational assistant for workplace productivity.

**Features:**
- ChatGPT-style interface
- Real-time responses
- Conversation history (session-based)
- Typing/loading indicators
- Supports writing, planning, summarising, and research help
